under a contract between claimant and Creekside Builders, who was the General Contractor in charge of the
construction of that certain improvement known as "5801 NE Voyage Avenue, Lincoln City” situated upon certain
land in Lincoln County, State of Oregon, which is the site of the improvement, described as follows:
Palisades Condominiums, Common elements, and as further described in the document records at Lincoln County
within the instrument found in book 125 and on page 2138.
The address of the improvement is (if unknown, so state): 5801 NE Voyage Avenue, Lincoln City.
The Name of the owner(s) or reputed owner(s) of the land and improvement are Palisades Condominium
Association. The name of the person who employed claimant, or to whom labor, materials, and/or equipment were
furnished is Creekside Builders.
The person(s) just named, at all times herein mentioned, and had knowledge of the construction.
Claimant commenced performance of the contract on January 14, 2008 provided and furnished all materials, labor
and equipment required by the contract and actually used in the construction of the improvement, and fully
completed the contract on January 14, 2008 after which claimant ceased to transport or furnish labor, equipment
and/or materials for the improvement.

owner on NA. This lien covers only those materials provided after a date which is eight days, not including
Saturdays, Sundays and other holidays, as defined in ORS 187.010, before the Notice of the Right to a Lien was
delivered or mailed.
